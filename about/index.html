Advent Hotel International will acquire, through a demerger process, valor Estates hospitality portfolio of 484 operating keys and identified land parcels and contracts enabling the rollout of 3,803 future keys.

The demerger process is expected to be completed within the next 10-12 months.

Following the demerger, Advent is expected to be widely held, with 47% held by existing Advent promoters and the remainder by public shareholders.

Advent will have its own corporate identity, capital structure, board, management, and branding.

Over the next 3-4 years, Advent will focus on operationalising 5 large upscale branded assets in key hospitality micro-segments in Mumbai and Delhi.

We are dedicated to becoming a leading player in the hospitality industry with a portfolio of luxury and upscale hotels across key markets.
